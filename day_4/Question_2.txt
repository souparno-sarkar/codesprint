Problem Statement
Souvik is building a secure digital firewall for CodeSprint 3.0.
To test the firewall strength, he designed a special numeric pattern.

The firewall follows these rules:

Numbers increase from 1 on the left side.

Numbers decrease on the right side.

The middle section is filled with @ symbols.

As the rows increase:

the number section grows

the @ section shrinks

Help Souvik generate the firewall pattern.

Input Format
A single integer N

Output Format
Print the required firewall pattern.

Example
Input
N = 5

Output
1@@@@@@@@1

12@@@@@@21

123@@@@321

1234@@4321

1234554321

Explanation:

1. Row 1 → numbers are small, so many `@`

2. With every row:

* left numbers increase

* right numbers mirror them

* middle `@` symbols decrease

Constraints
1≤N≤20

Hints (2)
1
Hint 1 : Every row has 3 parts — left numbers, middle `@`, right numbers.

2
Hint 2 : In each new row, the `@` symbols decrease by `2`.
